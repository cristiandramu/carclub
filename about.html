Our Anual Show

In over a decade, BAVARIANWORX has introduced completly new standard to the whole automotive world. The annual gathering created for enthusiasts is highlighting best builds of custom car industry and most precious automotive gems found all over Europe. What differs the show from other events, is fact of strict selection process for cars joining the exhibition. Only the finest builds will have a chance to find the spot among a thousand carefully chosen cars. The formula of the show is relativley uncomplicated and based on the simple aesthetic forms inspired by art and street culture. International format of the show is measured trough the thousands of the kilometers that drivers take to be part of the exhibition. At the same time the distance is the best reccomendation for the ones who never before experienced the Ultrace atmosphere. Despite ten years of experience, we are still perceived as a fashionable and very fresh event. Ultrace is the only and the ultimate opportunity to admire personalized cars from all over Europe, enjoy drifting at its best, and truly feel the bond with the street culture. Uncompromised focus on quality allowed us to create one of the most prestigous automotive events in the world. We are pleased that our consistenc in action has been recognized by the leading brands of the automotive industry. Without such significant support, we could not grow dynamically.
